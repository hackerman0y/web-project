Frequently Asked Questions
Is SkillSwap really free?
Yes, completely. There are no fees, no subscriptions, and no hidden charges. You trade skills, not money.
How do Skill Coins work?
You earn Skill Coins by teaching others on the platform. You can then spend them to learn from anyone — even if they don't need your specific skill.
What if someone doesn't show up?
You can report no-shows through the platform. Our Trust Score system keeps accountability high — repeat offenders are flagged and reviewed.
Can I teach online and offline?
Absolutely. Sessions can be online via video call or in-person — you and your swap partner decide what works best.
How is the Trust Score calculated?
After every session, both parties leave a review. Your Trust Score is built from these ratings — it reflects your reliability and teaching quality.
